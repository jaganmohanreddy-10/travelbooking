Flight Rules & Regulations

Review important flight policies and guidelines before you travel with Sky Wings.

Reporting Time

Report 2 hours before departure for domestic flights and 3 hours before departure for international flights.

Baggage Rules

Cabin baggage allowed up to 7 kg, check-in baggage allowed up to 25 kg. Extra charges apply for overweight luggage.

Cancellation Policy

Cancellation before 24 hours receives a partial refund. Cancellation within 24 hours carries standard cancellation charges.

Refund Policy

Refunds are processed within 5–7 working days to the original payment method after the cancellation is approved.

Passenger Guidelines

Carry valid ID proof, keep your boarding pass ready, follow airport security instructions, and switch electronic devices to flight mode onboard.
